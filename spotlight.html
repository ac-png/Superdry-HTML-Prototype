WOMEN'S JACKETS & COATS

The latest collection of Superdry jackets and coats for women offers endless style for your cold-weather closet this season. We obsess over these outerwear designs that seamlessly weave signature aesthetics with functional features. Discover our range of women's parka jackets and luxurious puffer jackets for when you need some premium insulation this season in the colder weather. The iconic Superdry windcheater has been re-designed in new colourways for the new winter season that will have you longing for a cold snap. Women's leather jackets are the ideal solution for the transitional period, offering beautifully crafted jackets in premium leather. Shop our range of women’s bomber jackets that seamlessly weaves signature aesthetics with functional features, or go for a women's longline coat for premium style and warmth this winter.
